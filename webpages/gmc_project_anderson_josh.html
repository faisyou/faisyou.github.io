Executive Project Highlight

GMC (UK) Support Modernisation

Migration from LivePerson to Genesys Cloud with AI-powered support across WhatsApp, Facebook, SMS, and WebMessaging for the UK’s medical regulator.

Context

About GMC (General Medical Council)

UK statutory regulator for doctors, Physician Associates (PAs) and Anaesthesia Associates (AAs), safeguarding patient safety and professional standards.

Project Snapshot

From Legacy Chat to AI-Driven Engagement

  • Migration: LivePerson → Genesys Cloud digital & AI platform
  • Channels: WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, SMS, WebMessaging
  • Agents: 75 agents supported with AI CoPilots
  • Coverage: Registration, revalidation, guidance & high-volume FAQs
AI & Automation

Virtual Agent & CoPilots

  • Virtual Agent: Intent classification & AI-powered slots
  • Knowledgebase: Policy-aligned answers with highlight & summarisation
  • CoPilots for Agents: Real-time suggestions, context and summarised histories
  • Escalation: Seamless handoff from bot to live agents when needed
Impact

Executive-Level Outcomes

  • Scalability: Automates routine queries across hundreds of thousands of practitioners.
  • Experience: 24/7, mobile-first access via WhatsApp & Facebook for busy clinicians.
  • Efficiency: Reduced handle times and load for 75 agents with AI assist.
  • Strategic Fit: Modern, data-driven regulatory service aligned with NHS digital ambitions.

This modernisation positions GMC to support growing registrant volumes (including PAs and AAs) while improving responsiveness, consistency and overall service quality.
