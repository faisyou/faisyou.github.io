GMC Support Modernisation
Executive Project Summary

GMC (UK) – AI-Enabled Support at Scale

Migration from LivePerson to Genesys Cloud, delivering AI-powered support for the UK’s medical regulator across WhatsApp, Facebook, SMS and WebMessaging.

Regulatory Context
GMC – General Medical Council

GMC is the statutory regulator for doctors, Physician Associates (PAs) and Anaesthesia Associates (AAs) in the UK, responsible for registration, standards, and fitness-to-practise.

Project Snapshot
Delivery on Genesys Cloud
  • Migration: LivePerson → Genesys Cloud digital & AI platform
  • Channels: WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, SMS, WebMessaging
  • Agents: 75 agents supported with AI CoPilots
  • Scope: High-volume queries on registration, revalidation and guidance

Omnichannel experience

Virtual Agent & CoPilots
AI Capabilities
  • Virtual Agent: Intent classification with AI-powered slots to collect key details (e.g., GMC reference, route, document type).
  • Knowledgebase: Answers sourced from approved content, with highlight & summarisation for clarity.
  • CoPilots: Real-time agent assist (summary, context, suggested responses) for 75 agents.
  • Handover: Seamless escalation from bot to live agents for complex or sensitive cases.
Executive Outcomes
Key Value Drivers
  • Scalability: Automates routine questions across a registrant base of 390k+ doctors.
  • Experience: 24/7, mobile-first access via WhatsApp & Facebook for busy clinicians.
  • Efficiency: Reduced handle times and workload through AI-assisted workflows for 75 agents.
  • Strategic Fit: Modern, data-driven service aligned with NHS and UK digital health ambitions.
At-a-Glance Impact
A single AI-enabled platform to support hundreds of thousands of practitioners and the organisations that rely on GMC every day.
