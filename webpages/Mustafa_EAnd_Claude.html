About E& Egypt

Part of Emirates Telecommunications Group, E& Egypt is the third-largest mobile operator in Egypt with 99% population coverage. Operating since 2007, they serve 37 million customers and have invested over EGP 50 billion in network infrastructure, making them a cornerstone of Egypt's digital transformation.
